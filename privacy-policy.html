Data Collection and Usage

MindTock operates with a privacy-first local framework wherever possible. We do not maintain external centralized databases of user profiles. To deliver our core accountability challenges, the app utilizes the following native hardware permissions and features:

  • Physical Accelerometer Sensors (Pedometer): MindTock utilizes your phone's built-in step counter sensors entirely locally to track your 60-step physical movement penalty to quiet active alarms. This activity data is never sent to external servers.
  • Foreground Services & Exact Alarms: We request high-priority background scheduling authority to guarantee that your custom alarm profiles ring exactly at the user-defined minute, bypassing system power restrictions or sleep modes.
  • Photo Library Access: MindTock allows you to optionally select a photo from your device's gallery to use as your alarm screen background. This image is copied to and stored entirely within the app's local storage on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or have any access to this image.
  • App Updates: MindTock checks Google Play for available app updates when the app launches, using Google's official In-App Update service.

Third-Party Advertising & Tools

To keep this application sustainable and support monetization, our platform integrates trusted third-party advertising modules that may collect anonymous device metrics to deliver relevant ad campaigns:

  • Google AdMob: We utilize Google AdMob to display banner and full-screen interstitial ads exclusively within wait/penalty screens. AdMob may utilize anonymous mobile identifiers and advertising tracking tokens to serve and optimize ad display. MindTock does not request or share location data with AdMob.

Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device (alarm schedules, labels, sound preferences, background images) persists until you delete it within the app or uninstall MindTock. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Your Rights

You may delete your locally stored data at any time by removing it within the app or uninstalling MindTock. For questions about data processed by our third-party providers (e.g., AdMob), please refer to their respective privacy policies, or contact us directly.
