Data Collection and Usage

MindTock operates with a strict, privacy-first local framework. We do not maintain external centralized databases of user profiles or log raw personal background details. To deliver our core accountability challenges, the app utilizes the following native hardware permissions completely on-device:

  • Physical Accelerometer Sensors (Pedometer): MindTock utilizes your phone's built-in step counter sensors entirely locally to track your 60-step physical movement penalty to quiet active alarms. This activity data is never sent to external servers.
  • Foreground Services & Exact Alarms: We request high-priority background scheduling authority to guarantee that your custom alarm profiles ring exactly at the user-defined minute, bypassing system power restrictions or sleep modes.
